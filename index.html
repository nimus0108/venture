Come to Boston for a weekend to work on your tech start up ideas.

January 9th - 10th, 2016

Venture is Boston’s first hybrid hackathon + startup competition for high school and college students. We invite students from all over the world to Boston for one weekend for an experience that blends together the arts of hacking and bootstrapping a company. Over 24 hours, 350 participants will build and ship products, create business models and pitch, and ultimately compete in front of a panel of New England’s most respected angel investors for investments and prizes.

Applications open Sep 15

FAQ

What is Venture?

Venture is a mix between a hackathon and a startup competition. Participants will have 24 hours to come up with a business plan and build an MVP.

Where is Venture?

Venture will take place in Boston/Cambridge. Our specific venue will be announced soon.

When is Venture?

Venture will take place on January 9th and 10th, 2016. This is an overnight event so participants will stay at the venue overnight.

Who can apply?

Venture is open to college as well as high school students anywhere globally.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. Instead, you will be able to recieve some money for travel if you are accepted. Venture is a completely free event, made possible by our sponsors. Interested in sponsoring?

Is Venture a hackathon?

Venture is a hackathon/startup competition. This means that you will be judged not only on the product, but your business plans. Also, Venture will have journalists writing about our participants and their products onsite. Tech and Non-tech students are welcome to apply.

Travel reimbursements?

If you are accepted, we will provide partial travel reimbursements. More details of how to recieve reimbursements will be announced after you apply.

When can I apply?

Applications open on September 15th. For Leangap's 2015 summer program, under 50 applicants were accepted from a pool of 900 students. Applying early will give you a better chance of securing a spot, as well as travel costs.
